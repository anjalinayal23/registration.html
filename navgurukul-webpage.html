Welcome To Navgurukul

We are a non-profit dedication to bring affordable tech education to underprivileged girls in India.

Our Story:

Started in 2016 ,Navgurukul runs one-year residential course for students from underserved and diverse communities. Students after persuing our course are awarded Certificate in Software Programming. The MOU Certificate is issued by DGT, Government of India.

In last five years more than 630+ Students from marginalized areas have got jobs as Software Programs and have donated back more than 32 Lac in donation. Together, these alumni are earning more than 16 crores a year are now instead tax-payers and job-creators. More than 1000 students are currently learning in residential centers across Bangalore, Pune, Delhi, Tripura, Amravati and Dharmsala.

Our Vision:

We envision a world where everyone has access to an affordable world class education that enables them to live up to their highest potential.
